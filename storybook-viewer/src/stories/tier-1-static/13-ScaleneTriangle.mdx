# Scalene Triangle

A scalene triangle with three different side lengths. All angles are different. Depending on side ratios, the triangle may be acute or obtuse.

## Elements

| Element | Type | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| `<Triangle>` #acute | Primitive | Acute scalene (all angles < 90) |
| `<Triangle>` #obtuse | Primitive | Obtuse scalene (one angle > 90) |

## Props Exercised

- `kind="scalene"`, `a`, `b`, `c` — Three side lengths
- `x`, `y` — Position
- `rotation` — Rotation around centroid

## Side Convention

- `a` = side v0 to v1 (the base, laid along the X-axis)
- `b` = side v1 to v2
- `c` = side v2 to v0

## What This Validates

- `kind="scalene"` uses three independent side lengths
- Vertex positions are computed via law of cosines
- The same component handles both acute and obtuse cases
