# 047 — Regular Pentagon

A regular pentagon (5 equal sides, 5 equal angles of 108 degrees) defined by circumradius. Uses the `Pentagon` component (thin wrapper over `Polygon` with `sides=5`).

## Elements

| Element | Type | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| `<Pentagon>` | Primitive | Regular pentagon |

## Props Exercised

- `r` — Geometry (circumradius)
- `centerX`, `centerY` — Center position
- `fill`, `stroke` — Presentation
- `rotation` — Rotation around center

## Default

<LiveCanvas code={`<Sprite viewBox="0 0 200 200">
  <Pentagon

    fill="#9b59b6"

</Sprite>`} />

## What This Validates

- `Pentagon` is sugar for `Polygon` with `sides=5`
- `r` (circumradius) determines size directly
- All 5 vertex anchors are registered
- Rotation applies around the center point
