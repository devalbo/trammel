# 048 — Regular Hexagon

A regular hexagon (6 equal sides, 6 equal angles of 120 degrees) defined by side length. Uses the `Hexagon` component (thin wrapper over `Polygon` with `sides=6`).

## Elements

| Element | Type | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| `<Hexagon>` | Primitive | Regular hexagon |

## Props Exercised

- `sideLength` — Geometry
- `centerX`, `centerY` — Center position
- `fill`, `stroke` — Presentation
- `rotation` — Rotation around center

## Key Hexagon Properties

For a regular hexagon with `sideLength = 40`:
- Circumradius (r) = sideLength = 40
- Apothem = `sideLength * sqrt(3)/2` = 34.64
- Width (flat-bottom) = `2 * sideLength` = 80
- Height (flat-bottom) = `2 * apothem` = 69.28

## What This Validates

- `Hexagon` is sugar for `Polygon` with `sides=6`
- `sideLength` correctly derives circumradius and apothem
- Flat-bottom orientation places a horizontal edge at the bottom
- Rotation applies around the center point
